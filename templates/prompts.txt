Start with both hands in a relaxed position at your chest, palms facing down.
Lift your right hand and move it upward and to the right, stopping at your right shoulder.
Lower your right hand to your chest, and then lift your left hand and move it upward and to the left, stopping at your left shoulder.
Lower your left hand to your chest, and then bring both hands together in front of your chest, with your thumbs touching and your fingers spread apart.
Keeping your thumbs touching, move both hands outward and away from your chest, ending with your arms extended and your hands about shoulder width apart.
